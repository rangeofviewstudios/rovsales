1
👋Opener

Warm, personal, acting as a genuine customer. Cater the detail to their page — their dish, the patio, a specific reel. Don't send the same line to everyone.

You · DM
Hey! Just followed you. Had to stop scrolling because your page made me hungry 😂 Do people usually order online, or is most of your business dine-in?
Goal: get a reply. Low-stakes, genuine, no pitch.
2
🌉Bridge

"DoorDash / third party"

"Our own site"
